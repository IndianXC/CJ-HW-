Join us at Sheep Time, where we offer a menu composed entirely of high-quality lamb products. Everything on the menu comes from a local grass, fed sheep. Of course we have standard offerings like high-quality lamb chops, leg of lamb and rack of lamb.

We also have more exotic offerings like rocky mountain oysters, lambs heart stew and a delicious tangy kiefer made from ewe's milk.
